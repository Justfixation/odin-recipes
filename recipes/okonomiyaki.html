Okonomiyaki

A prevalent Japanese streetfood

Where american pancakes are made with sugar and topped with syrup, Okonomiyaki is a savory meat-cabbage pancake topped with seaweed and fish flakes

Ingredients:

  • 200 ml water
  • 1 tsp Konbu dashi stock powder
  • 160g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 480 g finely chopped cabbage
  • 40g chopped green shallot
  • 40g Tenkasu
  • 160g thinly sliced pork/bacon
  • bonito flakes
  • Aonori
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Okonomyaki Sauce

Steps:

  1. Dissolve the dashi powder into the water to make the dashi stock.
  2. Place the flour in a large bowl and add salt, sugar, and pour the dashi stock in to mix. Set aside.
  3. Divide the chopped cabbage, green shallots, and tenkasu into 4 small mixing bowls.
  4. Add one egg each and 1/4 of flourand dashi mixture to each bowl.
  5. Add one egg each and 1/4 of flour and dashi mixture to each bowl.
  6. Mix them well together with a fork.
  7. Heat cooking plate to 200 degrees and pour the cabbage mixture onto the cooking plate to spread approximately 2cm thickness.
  8. Place the thinly sliced pork/bacon and bonito flakes on top of the cabbage mixture.
  9. When the edge of the okonomiyaki bad become firm and cooked, flip it over with two egg flippers.
  10. Put the lid on and steam grill for a few minutes.
  11. Turn the okonomiyaki over one more time to check whether the meat is cooked.
  12. Brush the okonomiyaki with Oknomiyaki Sauce and garnish with more bonito flakes/aonori to serve.
